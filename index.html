Jungle Math Runner

How to Play

A target number is shown at the top of the screen.

Multiples mode: run into numbers that are multiples of the target.
Factors mode: run into numbers that divide the target evenly.

✅ Correct numbers give points and build your combo.
❌ Wrong numbers, 🪵 logs, and 🌿 vines cost a life. You have 3 lives!
🤸 Jump over logs — tumble roll under vines.
⚠️ Letting a correct number slip past costs 5 points.

🌴 Casual mode: a calm, steady pace with no lives — wrong answers just cost points. Run as long as you like and press ✖ when you're done.

◀ ▶ or swipe — change lane
SPACE or swipe up — jump
▼ or swipe down — tumble roll
P / ESC — pause

Tip: you can jump over a wrong number to avoid it!
